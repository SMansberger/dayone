Meet

      Welcome and thank you for taking the time to check out our site, although our main piece of the site is centered around clothing we want you to know that clothing is not the main idea of the Day One brand. Part of life is waking up getting dressed and going on your day so what a better way to express yourself than through what you wear?

       We believe as we keep progressing forward we will be able to keep making more quality clothing each time. Some being very simple designs while others being more complex with a meaning behind it. We don't want to put a limit on what kind of clothes we make but fashion is just one way to express yourself today. With this blog and day one lifestyle as a whole, we plan to make numerous videos on people aspiring for bigger things in life and businesses in the community. Maybe it will be something you can relate too! Photography will also play a major part with our site in the future. Like they say, "a picture is worth a thousand words."

       I wanted to make the first video post a video that a friend and myself made a few months ago. While we had no plans of using it as a piece for day one I think it turned out to be something that really shows what day one is about. Gwen, who is also a member of the day one team has helped me grow as a person and also my video/photography skills. I think in the near future you can expect more videos like this, so be sure to keep up to date and thank you again,

Garrett.
